Who We Are

A Legacy of
Fine Craft

Oriental Artifacts is a Chiang Mai–based handicraft business with over 30 years of experience serving international customers. Specializing in authentic handmade carpets, textiles, jewelry, and cultural artifacts.

The store showcases pieces crafted by skilled artisans across Asia and the Middle East. Each item reflects tradition, heritage, and timeless craftsmanship, making Oriental Artifacts a trusted destination for those seeking meaningful and high-quality handcrafted goods.
