Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

We respect your privacy. This page explains what personal information we collect through our website, how we use it, and how we keep it safe. We do not sell products online — this website exists so you can explore our collection and get in touch with us directly.

Who We Are

Oriental Artifacts is a curated collection of handcrafted goods based in Chiang Mai, Thailand. We source authentic artifacts, textiles, and decorative pieces from skilled artisans across Southeast Asia and beyond.

If you have any questions about this policy, please reach out via our contact form.

What Information We Collect

We only collect personal information that you choose to provide through our contact form. This may include your name, email address, phone number, and the message you send us.

We do not collect any payment information, as we do not process sales through this website.

How We Use Your Information

The information you submit via our contact form is used solely to respond to your inquiry. We may follow up by email or phone to discuss your interest in our products.

We will never use your contact details to send unsolicited marketing messages, and we will never share or sell your personal information to third parties.

How We Store Your Information

Your inquiry details are handled securely and accessed only by our team. We retain your contact information only for as long as necessary to handle your inquiry and any follow-up communication.

Cookies & Analytics

Our website does not currently use tracking cookies or third-party analytics tools. If this changes in the future, this policy will be updated accordingly.

Your Rights

You have the right to ask what personal information we hold about you, to request that we correct or delete it, or to ask us to stop using it. To make any such request, please contact us through our contact page.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a revised date at the top.
